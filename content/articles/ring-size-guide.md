Ring sizing issues are one of the easiest problems to prevent with a few simple checks before ordering.

## Measure at the right time

Finger size changes with temperature and activity. Measure in normal conditions, ideally later in the day.

## Account for band width

Wider bands feel tighter. If you choose a broad design, test a half size up as part of your shortlist.

## Validate with a second method

Use both a ring sizer and an existing well-fitting ring reference to reduce guesswork.
