Lab-grown diamonds can offer strong value when evaluated with the same discipline used for natural stones.

## Certification first

Prioritize reputable lab reports so your comparisons are based on trusted grading rather than marketing language.

## Cut drives sparkle

Cut quality influences brilliance more than headline carat alone. Compare proportions and light return whenever possible.

## Compare total ring design

A well-designed setting can make a modest center stone look premium. Budget for both stone and craftsmanship.
