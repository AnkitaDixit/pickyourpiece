Diamond shape impacts both visual identity and value perception, sometimes more than buyers expect.

## Sparkle patterns differ

Round brilliant is known for consistent sparkle, while fancy shapes can emphasize length, silhouette, and unique character.

## Coverage and proportion

Oval, marquise, and pear often appear larger face-up than round stones of similar carat weight.

## Style compatibility

Match shape with setting style and hand profile. A shape that looks great alone may feel different once set.
