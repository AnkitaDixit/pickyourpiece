## 1. What Is Rose Gold?

Rose gold is an alloy made by mixing pure gold with copper (and sometimes small amounts of silver).

The copper content gives rose gold its pinkish-warm tone and contributes to its strength for daily wear.

---

## 2. Who Does Rose Gold Suit Best?

Rose gold is versatile, but it often stands out most on:

- Warm undertones
- Neutral undertones
- Medium to deeper skin tones

It can also look excellent on cool undertones when paired with the right stone and styling.

Practical tip: if yellow gold feels too rich and white gold feels too stark, rose gold is often the sweet spot.

## 7. Rose Gold Care and Maintenance

Rose gold is relatively low-maintenance, but basic care keeps it looking its best:

- Clean gently with mild soap and soft brush
- Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaners
- Remove during heavy gym/manual tasks
- Store separately to reduce scratches

Simple routine care usually preserves shine and detail for years.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is rose gold good for engagement rings?

Yes. Rose gold is a popular engagement ring choice for its warm tone, flattering look, and practical durability.

---

### Does rose gold fade over time?

Rose gold does not usually need rhodium replating like white gold. It may develop subtle tonal softness with wear, which many buyers enjoy.

---

### Is rose gold expensive?

Pricing varies by purity, weight, and design. In many cases, rose and yellow gold sit in similar ranges, while platinum is often priced higher.

---

### Can rose gold be resized?

Most rose gold rings can be resized, depending on design complexity and stone setting. Confirm resize policy before ordering.
