Rose gold combines warmth and softness, making it popular for contemporary as well as vintage-inspired designs.

## Color character

Its copper alloy creates a warm tone that pairs especially well with romantic and minimalist designs.

## Daily wear considerations

Rose gold generally ages gracefully, but regular cleaning helps maintain brightness and detail visibility.

## Best pairing choices

It pairs beautifully with white stones for contrast and with champagne tones for a softer palette.
