# Mistakes to Avoid When Buying an Engagement Ring

Buying an engagement ring is emotional, and that is exactly why common mistakes happen.

This guide shows the biggest errors and how to avoid each one.

---

## Quick Answer

Most costly mistakes are:

- No budget cap
- Overweighting carat over cut
- Choosing trend over long-term wearability
- Skipping policy and size checks

## 2. Mistake: Chasing Carat Alone

Carat without cut quality rarely looks better.

| Priority | Why It Matters |
|---------|-----------------|
| Cut | Sparkle and life |
| Shape | Face-up size perception |
| Setting | Overall ring balance |
| Carat | Only one part of value |
