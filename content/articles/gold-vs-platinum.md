Gold and platinum both work beautifully for rings, but they differ in weight, maintenance, and pricing structure.

## Durability behavior

Platinum is dense and displaces metal rather than wearing away quickly, while gold alloys may lose tiny amounts over years of polishing.

## Color and upkeep

White gold often needs periodic rhodium replating. Platinum develops a patina that many buyers find elegant and easy to maintain.

## Budget and value

Gold typically has a lower entry price and broad design options. Platinum can justify higher cost for buyers wanting weight and premium feel.
