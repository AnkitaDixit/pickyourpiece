Good budgeting is less about a fixed rule and more about aligning ring priorities with ownership comfort.

## Split your budget intentionally

Define percentages for center stone, setting, and service add-ons so you can compare quotes consistently.

## Spend on visible impact

Cut quality and design coherence often create more perceived value than small upgrades in less visible specs.

## Leave room for lifecycle costs

Plan for resizing, maintenance, and insurance so the purchase remains comfortable after checkout.
