## 3. Budget Split That Works

Use this practical split:

- 55% to 70%: center stone/look
- 25% to 35%: setting and metal
- 5% to 10%: resizing and maintenance

If you want bigger visual impact, allocate more to cut and shape quality. Use the [diamond shapes guide](/articles/diamond-shapes-guide?utm_source=internal_article&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign=engagement_rings_under_50000&utm_content=section3_shapes).
