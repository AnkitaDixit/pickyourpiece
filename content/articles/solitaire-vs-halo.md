Solitaire and halo designs communicate very different aesthetics and ownership experiences.

## Visual profile

Solitaires feel clean and timeless. Halo settings create a larger visual footprint with added sparkle around the center.

## Maintenance profile

Halo designs can involve more small stones and prongs, which may require more frequent checks over time.

## Budget fit

Halo settings can create more perceived size at similar budgets. Solitaires can focus spend on center-stone quality.
