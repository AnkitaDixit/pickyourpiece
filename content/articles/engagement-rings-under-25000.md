## 4. Stone Strategy: Size Look vs Sparkle

At this budget, smart stone strategy beats chasing carat.

Prioritize in this order:

1. Cut quality and light return
2. Face-up shape that looks larger
3. Setting style that amplifies center look
4. Certification clarity when available

## 5. Smart Budget Split for ₹25,000

A practical split that works for most buyers:

- 45% to 60%: stone or visual center element
- 30% to 45%: metal and setting craftsmanship
- 5% to 10%: resizing and post-purchase adjustments

If you are deciding between two rings, pick the one with better finish and cleaner proportion, not the one with the biggest marketing spec list.

- Focusing only on carat, ignoring overall design proportion
- Buying first, comparing later
